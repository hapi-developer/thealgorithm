How to Play
  • Each turn you have 2 actions: move, attack, or fortify.
  • Click your selected unit again to Fortify (+1 shield blocks 1 hit).
  • Beacons (glowing) score at end of your turn if occupied.
  • Snipers can attack up to 2 tiles in straight lines.
  • Win by reaching 5 beacon points or eliminating all enemy units.
