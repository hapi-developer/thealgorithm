Prism Relay

Flip the lights, tune the spectrum, solve the grid.

A sleek lights-out puzzle with shimmering prisms. Tap a tile to flip itself and its neighbors, then blackout the board in as few moves as possible.

How to Play

  • Click a tile to flip it and the four adjacent tiles.
  • Turn every tile off to solve the puzzle.
  • Challenge mode scales the grid as you win.
